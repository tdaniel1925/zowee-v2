✦ FAQ

Questions we get
all the time

Everything you need to know before you start texting.

📱

Do I need to download an app?

Absolutely not — that's the whole point. Zowee works entirely through SMS. You text a number, and Zowee handles everything from there. No app to download, no account to create, no login to remember. If your phone can send a text message, it can use Zowee. Works on iPhone, Android, flip phones, any carrier worldwide.

⚡

How fast does Zowee actually respond?

Our average response time is under 60 seconds for most requests. Simple tasks like restaurant recommendations or quick research come back in 10–30 seconds. More complex tasks like booking a flight or running a deep comparison report may take 1–3 minutes. We guarantee a response within 5 minutes for any request, 24/7.

💳

Is there a limit on how many requests I can send?

No limits, ever. Both the Solo and Family plans include completely unlimited SMS requests. Send 5 texts a day or 50 — the price never changes. No per-task fees, no overage charges, no throttling. We believe in one flat rate for unlimited access, period.

🔒

Is my data and payment info secure?

Security is our top priority. All SMS communications are encrypted end-to-end. We never store your payment details — billing is handled by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified processor. Your conversation history is encrypted at rest and we never sell your data to third parties. You can request full data deletion at any time by texting "DELETE MY DATA."

🌍

Does Zowee work outside the US?

Yes! Zowee works in 40+ countries. You can text from anywhere in the world using your local number. International SMS rates from your carrier may apply, but Zowee itself charges no extra fees for international use. We currently support English, Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese, with more languages coming soon.

↩️

What if I want to cancel or get a refund?

Cancel anytime — no questions asked, no cancellation fees. Just text "CANCEL" or visit your account page. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee: if you're not satisfied for any reason within your first 30 days, we'll refund 100% of your payment, no hassle. The 7-day free trial doesn't require a credit card at all.
