10-Second Hook ⭐ Most Used

The Instant Attention Grabber

Use when you have 1 sentence to spark curiosity

Cold Prospect Networking Event Online / DM ~20 words

Script

"I help business owners and side hustlers build a recurring income stream by setting them up on a professional SMS platform — and they get paid every month just for referring others. Want to hear how it works?"

Target: ≤ 10 sec
💡

Rep Delivery Tips

✓

Lead with the outcome ("recurring income") before explaining the mechanism. People buy results, not features.

✓

End with a question — it opens the door for a longer conversation without feeling pushy.

⚡

Power move: Pause after "recurring income stream" — let the phrase land before continuing.

30 sec
30-Second Pitch

The Classic Elevator Script

Problem → Solution → Proof → CTA structure

Warm Lead Networking Event Follow-up Call ~75 words

Script

"Most people I talk to are either looking for a way to earn extra income or they already run a business and want a better way to reach their customers. ZOWEE solves both. It's a professional SMS marketing platform — and here's the twist: every rep who signs up earns a recurring monthly commission for every subscriber they refer. We've got reps making $500 to $2,000 a month just from referrals. Takes about 10 minutes to get set up. Want me to walk you through it?"

Target: ≤ 30 sec
💡

Rep Delivery Tips

✓

Open with "most people I talk to" — it's relatable and positions you as someone who's had many of these conversations (social proof by implication).

✓

Drop the income range ($500–$2,000) naturally — it creates a concrete anchor without sounding like a sales pitch.

✓

Slow down on "10 minutes to get set up" — it removes the biggest objection (time/effort) before they can raise it.

⚡

Power move: Adapt the opening to match their situation — if they're a business owner, lead with the SMS angle; if they're a side hustler, lead with income.

60 sec
60-Second Demo 🔥 Highest Conversion

The Full Story + Live Demo

Full narrative with a live platform walkthrough moment

Warm Lead Follow-up Call In-Person Meeting ~160 words

Script

Part 1 · Hook (0–10s)

"Quick question — do you know anyone who runs a local business or is looking for a legit side income? Because I've been helping people set up something that pays them every single month, and it takes less than 10 minutes."

Part 2 · Explain (10–35s)

"It's called ZOWEE — it's a professional SMS marketing platform. Businesses use it to text their customers, run promos, send updates. The platform itself is $29 a month. But here's where it gets interesting: as a rep, every person you sign up pays you a recurring commission. Not a one-time thing — every month, as long as they're a subscriber."

Part 3 · Proof + Demo (35–55s)

"I've got reps in my network making $800, $1,200, even $2,000 a month just from referrals. And the platform actually works — businesses see real results from SMS. [Pull out phone] Let me show you the dashboard real quick — this is what your subscribers see, and this is your commission tracker right here."

Part 4 · CTA (55–60s)

"I can get you set up in literally 10 minutes right now, or we can schedule a proper walkthrough. Which works better for you?"

Target: ≤ 60 sec
💡

Rep Delivery Tips

✓

The "do you know anyone" opener is intentionally indirect — it lowers resistance and lets them self-identify without feeling sold to.

✓

The phone pull-out in Part 3 is the most powerful moment — it makes the platform real and tangible. Practice this transition until it feels natural.

✓

The CTA gives two options — both move forward. Never ask "are you interested?" — always assume interest and offer a choice of next step.

⚡

Power move: Customize the income range to match what you've actually seen in your network — authentic numbers are more persuasive than generic claims.

📱

Demo tip: Pre-load your ZOWEE dashboard before the conversation so the phone pull-out is instant and smooth.
